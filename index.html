Who we are

The primary focus of this group is on wide range of cutting-edge solutions to various up-coming technological problems in integration, content synchronization, performance benchmarking, hard-core issues in small-powered devices, effective utilization of small-computing devices in education, energy-efficient solutions for low-powered devices, and other relevant areas. Apart from these, it also focuses on inter-disciplinary research and development in education and teaching pedagogy.
